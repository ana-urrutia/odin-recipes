Chicken Salad

Description

This filling recipe is perfect for lunch and super easy to make!

Ingredients

  • Rotisserie chicken, shredded
  • Grapes, halved
  • Mayo
  • Mustard
  • Onion powder
  • Salt
  • Pickle juice (trust me, it's the secret ingredient)

Steps

  1. Place shredded Rotisserie chicken in a Tupperware container.
  2. In a separate bowl, create a sauce by combing the mayo, mustard, pickle juice, onion powder and salt.
  3. Mix sauce into the shredded chicken until fully coated.
  4. Add grapes to the mixture.
  5. Refrigerate overnight.
