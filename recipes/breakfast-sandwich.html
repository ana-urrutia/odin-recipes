Breakfast Sandwich

Description

This is my spin on the classic Breakfast Sandwich. Hold the bacon and sausage, we're watching our saturated fat intake!

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs (3 if you're really hungry)
  • 2 slices of bread
  • Butter lettuce
  • Tomato slices
  • Cheese (I like Boar's Head American Cheese)
  • Mayo

Steps

  1. Cook your eggs how you like. Scrambled, over easy, medium, or -my personal favorite- over hard.
  2. Towards the end of cooking, place cheese slice one ggs so it melts.
  3. Toast your bread then spread mayo.
  4. Place lettuce on both slices of toast.
  5. Lay tomato on one toast slice.
  6. Place cooked eggs on the other slice.
  7. Smash it together and you've got yourself a breakfast sandwich!
